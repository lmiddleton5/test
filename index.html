Let's begin

No matter who you are, where you're from; whether you're starting University online or offline, you're already One of US! UCLan Students' Union Freshers week begins Sunday 13th September 2020! Freshers is the perfect opportunity to make the most of your free Students' Union membership and get involved in your student community. Whether you're a new student or returning, self isolating or just self catering, this year's Freshers week has something for everyone.

Don't miss out on any news leading up to Freshers and beyond! Sign-up now and be the first to know!
