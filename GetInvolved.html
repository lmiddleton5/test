Join a club

UCLan Activities offer over 35 different Sports Clubs open to all UCLan students. From cricket to cheerleading and dance to golf, there really is something for everyone! Some of our clubs are competitive, and some are just for fun. All abilities are welcome- whether you’re completely new to the sport, or you’re semi -pro, there’s a club for you. Our Sports Clubs have a great social calendar, and they’re an easy way to meet new friends at University. Become part of a UCLan Students’ Union Sports Club, and join the Red and Black Army! Memberships open 1st August 2020!

Join A society

Got a passion for something? We’ve got a society for that! UCLan Students’ Union has a wide range of societies to choose from - and the list is always growing. Whether your interest is political, religious or hobbyist, there’s probably a society that you’ll enjoy- and if there isn’t, we can help you create one!Joining a society is the perfect way to meet new people with the same interests as you, make friends and express yourself. It also looks great on your future cv! Memberships open 1st August 2020!

Represent your peers!

The Students’ Union is led by Students, for Students. This means that you have the chance to shape the Union and University by creating policy and helping create positive change on campus and in the community. We are committed to making sure that you're at the heart of what we do; which is why we have hundreds of Reps that make sure your views and student voice are heard.

Share your innovative ideas and petitions through our Big Ideas platform. Be a Course Rep, School President, a member of Students' Council or even run for an Full-time paid Officer role in the annual elections. Contact yourunion@uclan.ac.uk for further information.

get experience!

Our student-led Inspiring Projects are a way for you to develop your skills, give back to the community and meet new people. If you’re short of time for regular volunteering, get involved with our One-off Wednesday opportunities which run every week of term time. Or if you have an interest in media, our on-campus student media platform Pulse could be for you.

No experience necessary as we’ll provide all the training you need! We reward our volunteers with prizes and certificates as part of the Livesey Award – so they look great on your CV.

Contact suvolunteering@uclan.ac.uk for more information.

Social Sports

We offer a wide variety of recreational and social sport opportunities. Sessions that are just for fun, weekly leagues and one off tournaments , so there is something for everyone we welcome everyone of all abilities whether you are a “sporty type” or not. It is really easy to get involved and the majority of our sessions are free, with a small entry fee for teams wanting to enter leagues.

Contact CBrown16@uclan.ac.uk for further information.
